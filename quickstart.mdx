## Path A: Sales Automation

### Find Your First Prospects

1. Click **"Find Prospects"** in the sidebar
2. Enter your search criteria:
   - **Search Query**: e.g., "French bakeries in Paris"
   - **Location** (optional): "Paris, France"
   - **Max Results**: Start with 10

3. Click **"Start Search"**

<Info>
The search uses AI to find companies matching your criteria. Takes ~30 seconds.
</Info>

### Review Your Prospects

Once the search completes:
- View company details (name, website, industry)
- Check verified email addresses
- See enrichment scores

### Create Your First Campaign

1. Select prospects (checkbox on the left)
2. Click **"Create Campaign"**
3. Fill in campaign details:
   - **Campaign Name**: e.g., "Paris Bakeries Outreach"
   - **Email Subject**: Write or generate with AI
   - **Email Body**: Write or generate with AI

4. Click **"Launch Campaign"**

<Tip>
Use the **"Generate with AI"** button to create personalized emails automatically!
</Tip>

### Track Performance

Go to **Campaigns** to see:
- Emails sent
- Open rates
- Click rates
- Replies

## Path B: AI Brand Monitoring

### Create Your First Project

1. Go to **Analytics** > **AI Search**
2. Click **"+ New Project"**
3. Enter your domain (e.g., `mycompany.com`)
4. Give it a name (optional)
5. Click **"Create Project"**

### Analyze Your Domain

1. Click **"Analyze Domain"**
2. Wait ~20 seconds while AI extracts:
   - Company name and industry
   - Services and products
   - Location and keywords
   - Potential competitors

3. Review the analysis

<Info>
This step uses GPT-5-mini to understand your business automatically.
</Info>

### Generate Test Prompts

1. Click **"Generate Prompts"**
2. Choose language (English, French, Spanish, etc.)
3. Wait ~10 seconds

You'll get **10 prompts** across categories:
- **Brand**: Direct questions about your company
- **Product**: Questions about your offerings
- **Competitor**: Comparison queries
- **Intent**: Discovery queries (most valuable!)

### Run Your First Analysis

1. Review and activate/deactivate prompts
2. Click **"Run Analysis"**
3. Select AI engines to test:
   - âœ… ChatGPT (GPT-5)
   - âœ… Claude 3.5 Sonnet
   - âœ… Gemini 2.5 Flash
   - âœ… Perplexity Sonar Pro
   - âœ… Mistral Large

4. Click **"Start Analysis"**

<Warning>
Running 10 prompts on 5 engines = 50 AI queries. This will use ~â‚¬5 of your credits.

### View Results

Once complete, you'll see:
- **Visibility Score**: % of responses mentioning your brand

- **Sentiment**: How AI talks about you
- **Competitors Mentioned**: Who AI recommends instead

## Step 3: Configure API Keys (Optional)

By default, tldr.work uses **shared API keys** so you can start immediately.

To use your **own API keys** (lower costs, more control):

1. Go to **Settings** > **API Keys**
2. Add your keys:
   - **Firecrawl** (for prospect search)
   - **Hunter.io** (for email enrichment)
   - **OpenRouter** (for AI analysis)
   - **Resend** (for email sending)

<Tip>
Using your own keys can reduce costs by up to 50%!
</Tip>
