## Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Click "Analyze Domain"

In your project page, click **"Analyze Domain"**

### Step 2: AI Scrapes Website

our discovery engine extracts:
- Homepage content
- About page
- Services page
- Metadata (title, description)

**Time**: ~20 seconds

### Step 3: AI Extracts Info

GPT-5-mini analyzes content and structures data.

**Time**: ~10 seconds

### Step 4: Review Results

</Frame>

- Company name

---

## Editing Analysis

### Why Edit?

- Add niche keywords AI missed
- Include competitor aliases (e.g., "HubSpot CRM" not just "HubSpot")
- Refine description for clarity
- Add location if not detected

### How to Edit

1. Click **"Edit"** next to any field
2. Modify text
3. Click **"Save"**

Changes apply to future prompt generation.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Analysis failed">
    **Reasons**:
    - Domain doesn't exist
    - Website is down
    - Robots.txt blocks scraping
    
    **Solutions**:
    - Check domain spelling
    - Verify website is accessible
    - Try again in a few minutes
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Extracted info is wrong">
    **Why**: Homepage doesn't clearly explain your business
    
    **Solutions**:
    - Edit manually
    - Update your website content
    - Re-analyze after website update
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="No competitors suggested">
    **Why**: Niche industry or new market
    
    **Solutions**:
    - Add competitors manually
    - Research competitors on Google
    - Ask ChatGPT: "Who are the top competitors of [your company]?"
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
