## Competitive Benchmarking

### Example Report

| Brand | Mentions | Visibility | Avg Position |
|-------|----------|-----------|--------------|
| **Your Brand** | 20 | 40% | #2.0 |
| Competitor A | 35 | 70% | #1.5 ⭐ |
| Competitor B | 28 | 56% | #2.2 |
| Competitor C | 15 | 30% | #4.0 |

**Insights**:
- Competitor A dominates - study their content
- You rank above Competitor C - good positioning
- Competitor B is close - monitor them

## Gap Analysis

### Prompts Where You're Missing

See queries where competitors appear but you don't:

**Example**:
```
Prompt: "Best CRM for startups"

✅ Mentioned: Salesforce (#1), HubSpot (#2), Pipedrive (#3)
❌ Not mentioned: Your brand
```

**Action**: Optimize content for "CRM for startups" keyword

### Engines Where Competitors Win

**Example**:
```
Claude mentions:
- Competitor A: 8/10 prompts
- You: 2/10 prompts

Action: Study what Claude values (citations, detailed answers)
```

## Learning from Competitors

### Content Strategy

Visit competitor websites and note:
- **Keywords they use**: What terms appear frequently?
- **Page structure**: How is content organized?
- **Use cases**: What industries do they target?
- **Social proof**: Case studies, testimonials, client logos

### AI Optimization Tactics

Check if competitors:
- Have FAQ pages (AI loves structured Q&A)
- Publish blog posts (more content = more visibility)
- Have comparison pages ("vs Competitor" pages)
- Get mentioned in directories/review sites

---

## Tracking Competitors

### Create Competitor Projects

1. Go to **Analytics** > **AI Search**
2. Create project with competitor domain
3. Use same prompts as your own analysis
4. Compare results

**Benefits**:
- Track competitor visibility over time
- See their strengths/weaknesses
- Identify opportunities to overtake them
