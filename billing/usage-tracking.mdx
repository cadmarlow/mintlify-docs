# Usage Tracking

tldr.work tracks billable usage so you always know where your budget is going.

---

## What Is Logged

For each billable operation, the platform records:

- operation type (AI Search, enrichment, email generation, email sent, etc.)
- service category
- cost charged
- timestamp

This data powers your Billing dashboard views.

---

## Where to See Usage

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing">
    In the app, click your avatar in the top-right, then click **Billing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Usage This Cycle">
    See total spend for the current cycle and a breakdown by service.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Recent Activity">
    See line-by-line operations with individual costs and timestamps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch period">
    Use the period filter (7d / 30d) to inspect recent trends.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How to Use This Data

- Identify which workflows consume most budget.
- Estimate monthly spend before scaling campaigns or analyses.
- Upgrade your plan proactively if your current budget is consistently exhausted.
