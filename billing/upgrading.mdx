# Upgrading and Cancellation

You can manage your billing directly from the app and Stripe portal.

---

## Upgrade Your Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing">
    Click your avatar in the top-right, then click **Billing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a plan">
    In the Billing page, select the plan you want (Starter, Pro, Ultra).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete checkout">
    You will be redirected to secure Stripe checkout to confirm your subscription.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
If you are on free credits and running out of balance, upgrading immediately restores usable monthly budget.
</Tip>

---

## Manage Subscription in Stripe

From Billing, you can open **Manage subscription** to access Stripe portal and:

- update payment method
- download invoices
- review active subscription details
- cancel subscription

---

## Cancellation Policy

- You can cancel at any time via Stripe portal.
- No long-term lock-in.
- No refunds for already used time/credits, except where legally required.

| Plan | Best when |
|------|-----------|
| Starter (€20) | Light monthly usage, solo testing and small pipelines |
| Pro (€60) | Ongoing weekly usage and multi-feature workflows |
