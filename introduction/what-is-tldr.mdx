# What is tldr.work?

tldr.work is **the operating system for your business** - an all-in-one platform that combines AI-powered sales automation with brand monitoring across AI engines like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini.

## The Problem We Solve

Modern businesses face two critical challenges:

### Challenge 1: Sales is Time-Consuming

- Finding qualified prospects takes hours
- Writing personalized emails is repetitive
- Tracking campaigns requires constant monitoring
- Scaling outreach means hiring more people

### Challenge 2: AI is Changing Discovery

- 60% of searches now happen on AI assistants (ChatGPT, Perplexity, etc.)
- Traditional SEO doesn't work for AI engines
- You have no idea if AI recommends your brand
- Competitors might be ranking higher than you

## The tldr.work Solution

We combine **two powerful engines** in one platform:

## How It Works: Sales Automation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Prospects">
    Enter what you're looking for (e.g., "SaaS companies in Paris"). AI finds matching companies automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enrich Data">
    Get verified email addresses, LinkedIn profiles, and company details for each prospect.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Emails">
    AI writes personalized emails for each prospect based on their company, industry, and role.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Campaign">
    Send emails automatically with smart scheduling and tracking.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track Results">
    See opens, clicks, and replies in real-time. AI learns what works best.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How It Works: AI Brand Monitoring

<Steps>
  <Step title="Analyze Your Domain">
    Enter your website. AI extracts your business info (industry, services, competitors).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Test Prompts">
    AI creates 10 realistic queries people might ask (e.g., "Best CRM for startups").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test 5 AI Engines">
    Run prompts on ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, and Mistral.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Analyze Visibility">
    See if AI mentions your brand, where you rank, and who it recommends instead.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Optimize">
    Use insights to improve your content and get recommended more often.
  </Step>
</Steps>
