### 1. AI-Powered Prospect Discovery

Find qualified leads automatically using natural language queries.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Smart Search" icon="magnifying-glass">
    - **Natural language**: "SaaS companies in Paris with 10-50 employees"
    - **Industry filtering**: Technology, Real Estate, Healthcare, etc.

    - **Company size**: Filter by employee count or revenue

    - Returns clean, structured data
  </Accordion>

### 2. Email Enrichment & Verification

Get verified email addresses for every prospect.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Email Finding" icon="envelope">
    - Searches public sources (websites, LinkedIn, directories)
    - Finds decision-makers (founders, CEOs, marketing heads)
    - Returns full name, role, email, and LinkedIn profile
    - Powered by **Hunter.io** (industry leader)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Email Verification" icon="shield-check">
    - **SMTP validation**: Checks if email exists
    - **MX record check**: Validates domain configuration
    - **Catch-all detection**: Identifies risky domains
    - **Verification score**: 0-100 (>90 = 98%+ deliverability)
    - **Disposable email detection**: Filters out temporary emails
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Info>
Verified emails have **98%+ deliverability** compared to 60-70% for unverified lists.
</Info>

Create personalized emails in seconds with GPT-5.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Contextual Personalization" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    - Uses prospect data: company name, industry, recipient role
    - Adapts tone: Professional, casual, or friendly
    - Customizes length: Short (100 words), medium (200), or long (300)
    - Generates subject line + body
    - **Context-aware**: Mentions relevant pain points for each industry
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Customization Options" icon="sliders">
    - **Tone**: Professional, casual, friendly
    - **Length**: Short, medium, long
    - **Goal**: Meeting request, demo offer, partnership proposal
    - **Add context**: Special offers, case studies, testimonials
    - **Regenerate**: Don't like it? Generate a new version
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Quality Control" icon="certificate">
    - Powered by **GPT-5** (latest model)
    - Avoids spam triggers (no ALL CAPS, excessive punctuation)
    - Natural language (reads like a human wrote it)
    - Proper grammar and punctuation
    - **Human-in-the-loop**: You review before sending
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
Always review AI-generated emails before sending. Add personal touches for best results!
</Warning>

### 4. Automated Campaign Management

Launch and track email campaigns effortlessly.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Campaign Setup" icon="rocket">
    - Select prospects from your database
    - Choose template or write custom email
    - Set daily sending limit (default: 50/day)
    - Schedule start date/time
    - Enable/disable tracking
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Smart Scheduling" icon="calendar-days">
    - **Daily limits**: Prevent spam filters (50 emails/day recommended)
    - **Time zones**: Send at optimal times for each recipient
    - **Gradual sending**: Spreads emails throughout the day
    - **Pause/resume**: Stop campaign anytime
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Tracking & Analytics" icon="chart-line">
    - **Opens**: Who opened your email (with timestamp)
    - **Clicks**: Which links were clicked
    - **Replies**: Auto-detected in your inbox
    - **Bounces**: Hard bounces (invalid) vs soft bounces (full inbox)
    - **Real-time dashboard**: See stats update live
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
**Best Open Rates**: Tuesday-Thursday, 10-11 AM or 2-3 PM in recipient's timezone.
</Tip>

    - **Visibility Score**: % of responses mentioning your brand (0-100%)
    - **Average Position**: Where you rank when mentioned (#1-10)
    - **Mentions**: Total number of times you're mentioned
    - **Sentiment**: Positive, neutral, or negative (% breakdown)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Breakdown by Category" icon="chart-pie">

    - **Product prompts**: Should be high (80-100%)
    - **Competitor prompts**: Tests ranking in "best of" lists
    - **Intent prompts**: Most important - organic discovery rate
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Breakdown by Engine" icon="layer-group">
